Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Patrick Automation is a personal creator productivity tool that integrates with TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Basic profile info (username, display name, follower count, like count)
  • Video list and analytics (views, likes, comments, shares)
  • Content posting permissions if authorized by you

How We Use Your Information

  • Display your TikTok analytics within our personal dashboard
  • Enable content posting on your behalf when you request it
  • Help you track content performance

Data Storage

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. All data is used exclusively for the account owner within Patrick Automation.

TikTok API Compliance

This application complies with TikTok API Terms of Service. Data is used only within the scope of permissions you grant.

Your Rights

Revoke access anytime via TikTok Settings under Manage App Permissions.
